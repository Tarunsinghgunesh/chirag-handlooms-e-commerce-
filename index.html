Our Story

Ek Family Ka Sapna,
Hazaaron Gharon Ki Khushiyan

1994 mein Bharatpur ke ek chhote se loom se shuru hua tha Chirag Handlooms ka safar. Hamari dadi ke haath se buna pehla bedsheet aaj bhi hamare ghar mein hai.

Aaj hum 3 generations se ye kaam kar rahe hain — local weavers ko rozgar dete hue, Rajasthan ki handloom tradition ko zinda rakh rahe hain, aur Bharatpur ke hazaaron ghar ko premium fabrics de rahe hain.

  • Bharatpur ke local weavers ka kaam hai
  • Natural, eco-friendly dyes use karte hain
  • Har product quality checked hota hai
  • Bulk orders ke liye special discount milti hai
  • Hotels, hostels aur businesses ke liye bulk supply
